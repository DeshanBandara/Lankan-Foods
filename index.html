What is sri lankan foods

Sri Lankan cuisine is rich in flavor and influenced by the country's history, geography, and diverse cultural heritage. The food of Sri Lanka is characterized by its aromatic spices, coconut, and use of rice as a staple ingredient.

  1. Rice and Curry: The centerpiece of Sri Lankan cuisine is a meal called "rice and curry," where steamed rice is served with an array of curries. These curries can include meat or fish, but there are also many delicious vegetarian options.
  2. Hoppers (Appa): These are bowl-shaped pancakes made from rice flour and coconut milk. There are two main types: plain hoppers and egg hoppers, which have an egg cracked into the center.
  3. Kottu Roti: A popular street food, kottu roti is made from shredded flatbread mixed with vegetables, egg, and your choice of meat or seafood. It's chopped and stir-fried on a hot griddle with a variety of spices.
  4. Lamprais: This is a Dutch-influenced dish where rice is mixed with a combination of meat (often beef, chicken, or pork), sambal (chili paste), and spices. The mixture is then wrapped in a banana leaf and steamed.
  5. Pol Sambol: A side dish made from freshly grated coconut, red onions, chili peppers, lime juice, and sometimes Maldive fish. It adds a spicy kick to meals and complements rice and curry dishes.
  6. Fish Ambul Thiyal: A sour and spicy fish curry made with tamarind, goraka (dried Garcinia cambogia fruit), and various spices. It's a unique and flavorful preparation.
  7. Wambatu Moju: An eggplant pickle cooked with spices, vinegar, and sugar, giving it a sweet and tangy flavor.
  8. Kiribath: A ceremonial dish of milk rice, traditionally served during important occasions like Sinhalese New Year. It's made by cooking rice in coconut milk and served with a variety of accompaniments.
  9. Pittu: A cylindrical-shaped steamed dish made from rice flour and grated coconut, often served with a curry.
  10. Roti: Sri Lankan roti is a type of flatbread, typically served with curry or used as a wrap for various fillings like vegetables, meat, or cheese.
